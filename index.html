About Me

Mikayla Vosseller is an avid equestrian that combines her passion for horse sports and marketing in her current role as the Marketing Manager at Equivont, an equestrian's one-stop-shop for products and services. Mikayla uses her digitial media and marketing skills to help small equestrian businesses elevate their online presence, whether it be through a new website or current social media.
