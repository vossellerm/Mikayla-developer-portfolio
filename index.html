About Me

Mikayla Vosseller is an avid equestrian that combines her passion for horse sports and marketing in her current role as the Marketing Manager at Equivont, an equestrian's one-stop-shop for products and services. Mikayla uses her digitial media and marketing skills to help small equestrian businesses elevate their online presence, whether it be through a new website or social media.

Mikayla's skills in Web Development include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and JQuery. These skills allow for the creation and deployment of functional websites and applications.
